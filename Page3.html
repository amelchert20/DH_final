Unofficial Antique Furniture Buying Guide

  1. The type of wood is often discernable through the finish via the grain pattern.
  2. Signs of age include shortness and marrs from past lives.
  3. Style is likely apparent through the craftsmanship and detail.
  4. The price should be appropriate for needed labor and parts as well as what it would sell for currently.
